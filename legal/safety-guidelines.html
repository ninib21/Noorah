For Parents

Before Booking

  • Review sitter's background check results
  • Check ratings and reviews from other parents
  • Verify certifications (CPR, First Aid)

Preparing for Care

  • Provide detailed emergency contact information
  • Share important medical information about your children
  • Leave clear instructions for routines, meals, and bedtime
  • Show sitter location of first aid kit and emergency supplies
  • Provide contact information for pediatrician and nearest hospital
  • Ensure sitter has your location and expected return time

During Care Sessions

  • Monitor real-time location updates through the app
  • Check in periodically via the app messaging system
  • Ensure sitter follows your instructions and house rules
  • Be available by phone in case of questions or emergencies

For Sitters

Professional Standards

  • Maintain current CPR and First Aid certifications
  • Complete all required background checks and verifications
  • Arrive on time and follow all parent instructions
  • Never leave children unattended
  • Do not invite guests or have visitors during care sessions
  • Avoid using personal devices excessively while caring for children

Safety Protocols

‚úÖ Session Start Checklist:

  • Confirm emergency contact information
  • Review any medical conditions or allergies
  • Locate first aid kit and emergency supplies
  • Understand house rules and routines
  • Check that doors and windows are secure
  • Ensure panic button is accessible on your phone

During Care

  • Supervise children at all times
  • Follow all safety rules (car seats, helmets, etc.)
  • Keep children away from potential hazards
  • Document any incidents or concerns immediately
  • Use the app to provide regular updates to parents
  • Never administer medication without explicit parent permission

Emergency Procedures

Medical Emergencies

  1. Call 911 immediately for serious injuries or medical emergencies
  2. Provide first aid if trained and safe to do so
  3. Contact parents immediately
  4. Use NannyRadar panic button to alert emergency contacts
  5. Document the incident in the app

Safety Threats

  1. Ensure immediate safety of children
  2. Call 911 if there is immediate danger
  3. Use NannyRadar panic button
  4. Move to a safe location if possible
  5. Contact parents as soon as it's safe to do so

Natural Disasters

  • Follow local emergency procedures
  • Move to designated safe areas (basement for tornadoes, etc.)
  • Keep emergency kit accessible
  • Monitor emergency broadcasts
  • Stay with children until parents return or emergency services arrive

Technology Safety

Location Sharing

  • Location is shared only during active sessions
  • Parents can view real-time location for safety
  • Location data is encrypted and secure
  • Historical location data is automatically deleted after 30 days

Communication

  • Use only NannyRadar messaging for communication
  • Do not share personal contact information
  • Report inappropriate messages immediately
  • All communications are monitored for safety
